Why people pick Active Guard

Recording is fine for evidence after the fact — but most people prefer the thing not to happen in the first place. This kit is built around that idea.

🚨

Siren and strobe on detection

When the camera spots a person where it shouldn't, the spotlights flash and the siren sounds. Most opportunistic visitors don't stick around.

🌈

Full-colour night vision

Sensor-and-light combo gives you real colour at night instead of the usual grainy black and white — clothing, vehicle colour and faces all readable.

🎙️

Two-way audio

Talk through the camera from your phone — useful for couriers, gates, or telling someone they're being recorded.

⏰

Schedule assistant

Set the deterrent to behave differently in the day vs overnight — e.g. silent recording during business hours, full siren response after close.

🧠

Value AI alerts

The on-board AI focuses on people and vehicles, so you get useful notifications instead of one ping every time a leaf moves.

📲

Full IVSEC X app control

Live view, playback, motion alerts, two-way talk and siren trigger — all from your phone, anywhere with data.
