Why pick Pro 4K Guard

The full Pro 4K kit with the active deterrent from the Active Guard line baked into the cameras. Best of both worlds — no trade-offs.

🚨

Siren + strobe on detection

Each camera fires its built-in siren and flashes its strobes when smart motion picks up a person. Most opportunistic visitors don't stick around — even less so with four cameras going at once.

🎯

4K detail at distance

8MP cameras pull faces, vehicle colour and number plates out of the frame where a 4MP camera would just show a blur — a real difference on larger blocks and longer driveways.

🌈

Full-colour night vision

Spotlight + sensor combo keeps colour info after dark — clothing, vehicles and faces stay identifiable in dark areas where a standard IR camera shows only shapes.

💾

4TB pre-installed

Double the storage of the standard kits. At 4 channels of 4K that typically means weeks of continuous footage with motion clips kept much longer.

🛡️

IP67 housing

Fully sealed against dust and capable of brief immersion. Built for unsheltered spots, exposed walls and harsher weather.

🧠

Heavy on the AI

Face recognition, plate detection, line-crossing, intrusion zones, queue length and crowd-density — all running on the recorder, not a cloud subscription.
