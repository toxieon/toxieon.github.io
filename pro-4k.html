Why pick Pro 4K

This is the kit you choose when you want the best version of the system — sharper picture, longer recordings and the tougher outdoor build.

🎯

4K detail at distance

8MP cameras pull faces, vehicle colour and registration plates out of the frame where a 4MP camera would just show a blur — a real difference on larger blocks and longer driveways.

💾

4TB pre-installed

Double the storage of the standard kits. At 4 channels of 4K that typically means weeks of continuous footage with motion clips kept much longer.

🛡️

IP67 housing

One step above IP66 — fully sealed against dust and capable of brief immersion. Built for unsheltered spots and harsher weather.

⚡

One-cable PoE+ install

One Cat6 run does both power and footage back to the NVR. No plug packs at each camera, cleaner walls, fewer points of failure.

🎙️

Two-way audio

Talk through the camera from your phone. Handy for couriers, gates, or letting visitors know they're being recorded.

🧠

Heavy on the AI

Face recognition, plate detection, line-crossing, intrusion zones, queue length and crowd-density — all running on the recorder, not a cloud subscription.
