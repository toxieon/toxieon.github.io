Two ways to enable that: (1) enable Google Sheets API on your Maps API key (auto-detect every tab); or (2) add a row to the Rates tab with Key=extra_tabs and Value=Cable, Conduit, Misc, GPOs, Switch (comma-separated list of every extras tab).
